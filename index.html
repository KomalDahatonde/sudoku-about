How to Play

To play Sudoku, simply click on a cell and input the number you think belongs there. The game will provide feedback if the number is correct or incorrect. Keep filling in numbers until the entire grid is complete.

you can delete the number if it is in wrong place.

you can add suggestions for your self and add external numbers.

Difficulty Levels

Sudoku puzzles come in different difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard. The difficulty is determined by the complexity of the initial setup and the strategies required to solve the puzzle.

you can play sudoku for 4x4 or 9x9.

And select difficulty level like Easy, Medium and Hard.

Importing Sudoku

If you have a Sudoku puzzle saved elsewhere, you can import it to our platform and start solving right away. Simply copy the puzzle and paste it into the import form.

Create Your Own Sudoku

Feeling creative? You can also create your own Sudoku puzzle using our interactive grid. Once you're satisfied with your creation, you can share it with friends or challenge yourself to solve it.
